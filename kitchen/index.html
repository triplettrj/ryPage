✨ What's new in My Kitchen
  • Cleaner Inventory — one view, storage layout cards only. No more duplicate flat list.
  • Unassigned items — items without a zone now show at the bottom so nothing is lost.
  • Need to restock — out-of-stock items surface in one place with a quick ↺ restock button.
  • Barcode scan hero — scan barcodes is now the big primary action on the Scan tab.
  • Meal planning in Cook — weekly plan lives inside the Cook tab, not a separate screen.
  • AI expiry dates — Claude suggests best-by dates for any food item, not just 40 hardcoded ones.
  • Cook → depletion review — after cooking, the app asks what you used and updates quantities.
  • AI shelf layout — photo your fridge or pantry and Claude maps the zones for you.
